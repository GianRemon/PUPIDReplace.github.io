Application Portal for New/Replacement Identification Card

At the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, we understand the importance of having a valid identification card for our students. Whether you're applying for a new ID or need a replacement, we're here to assist you.

Our ID card serves as your official identification within the university campus and grants access to various facilities and services.

To get started, please review the ID Replacement Price for applicable fees and the Procedure section to understand the steps involved in the application process.

If you're ready to apply, kindly fill out the Request Form and submit it online. For any inquiries or assistance, please contact our office using the details provided in the Contact Us section.
